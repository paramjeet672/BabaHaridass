About Us

Baba Haridass Institute of Nursing Education has been set up by the Jan Kalyan Society (Regd.) Head Office-Panipat as a legal jurisdiction place for all matters, with an aim to impart Nursing Education who aspire for becoming qualified professional nurses. The School of Nursing is running since 1999.


The aim of the Institute is to provide nursing education to those qualified girls who have aptitude for nursing and caring for the sick and infirm in this semi urban belt of Delhi. The Institute also emphasizes, that student nurses, while in their professional training, learn and practice morality and spirituality for providing devoted and sincere professional Nursing care to the patients for whom they care in hospitals, community and school health programmes with complete integrity and concern for welfare of the society at large.


The Institute of Nursing education is located on the main road connecting Najafgarh to Bahadurgarh with buses plying frequently from all parts of Delhi to Najafgarh and Jharoda Kalan. Institute of Nursing Education has a complete separate building with a nurses hostel. It is spread over an area of two acres of land in a scenic beauty of nature.

The service of suffering mankind is the true service of God. Nursing is one such noble profession where one can alleviate the pain and agony of the society. With the blessings of Shri Sai Baba and Baba Haridass ji, we are fully dedicated to the furtherance of spreading of this noble nursing education as guided and motivated by the Great Florence Nightingale.

-Sh. Kapoor Singh, Founder
