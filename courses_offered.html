Courses Offered

GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery Course)

    Duration: 3 years which includes six months internship. Students are admitted only once in a year.

    Eligibility:

    • The minimum educational requirement is class XII (pass).
    • The applicant at the time of admission must have completed the Age of 17 years and should not be above 35 years as on 31st December of the year in which admission is sought. Age relaxation for SC candidates as per rule.
    • Admission is subject to satisfactory medical examination report.


ANM (Auxillary Nurse & Midwife Course)

    Duration: 2 years. Students are admitted only once in a year.

    Eligibility:

    • 10+2 class passed or its equivalent preferably with Science subjects with aggregate of 50% marks. 5% marks relaxation for SC/ST candidates.
    • Those who have cleared 10+2 vocational ANM Course (revised after 2001) from school recognized by Indian Nursing Council.
    • The applicant at the time of admission must have completed the Age of 17 years and should not be above 35 years as on 31st December of the year in which admission is sought. For ANM/LHV, there is no age bar. Age relaxation for SC candidates as per rule.
    • Admission is subject to satisfactory medical examination report.
