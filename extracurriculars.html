Rules & Regulations

         General Directions

  • Stay in hostel is compulsory.
  • Fees are payable in advance. Fees and other payable in advance. Fees and other charges once paid by the candidate are not refundable in full or in part or any account whatsoever.
  • Ragging is strictly prohibited.
  • Academic:
    1. Students shall be punctual and regular for lectures, demonstrations and clinical experience. 75% attendance in theory is essential to be eligible to appear in DNC examination.
    2. Students missing clinical experience and community health experience are required to make it up during their holidays and vacations.
    3. Student should obtain atleast 50% marks in the test conducted in the Institute.
    4. The discipline of the Institute is to be strictly observed.

    Any act of misbehavior, violation of rules, refusal from duty, poor performance in theory and practical, instigations for strikes and misconduct will be viewed seriously which can result in expulsion from the institute without any claims.

  • Students shall pay for all damages caused by them to the Institution property and library books.
  • Students shall not keep with them valuables such as jewellery, cash etc. Authorities will not be responsible for any loss.
  • All students must be member of the student nurses Association and contribute their talents to this professional organization. The yearly SNA membership has to be paid at the starting of the new session. Participation in extracurricular activities is compulsory for all students.
  • Institute does not have any kind of responsibility of the student while going to the hospital community experience or while going and coming back from home.
  • No students will leave the hostel or hospital without permission.
  • Students will not be allowed to entertain their parents / guardian / relations / friends during school hours.
  • Students are forbidden to accept gifts (in kind or cash) from the patients and their relatives.
  • Intimacy with the male persons in the hospitals / Field Areas will be viewed seriously and may result in termination of Training.
  • During the training period, no student will be permitted to appear in any other examination or competition.
  • The Principal / Director reserves the right to change the time table and the yearly planner at any time.
  • In all matters, the decision of the PRINCIPAL will be final.
  • Students should always be in complete, clean and neat uniform. Hostel rules have to be observed very strictly by all students.
  • The student should follow the rules and regulations of the institute regarding attendance, marks, dress code, SNA and leave policy. The details of the same can be obtained from the class coordinator at the time of joining the Institute. Student may be expelled if she disobeys rules and regulations prescribed by the authority.

Note: Students shall abide by all the rules and regulations and such other instructions as may be issued from time to time. Rules are subject to change in accordance with decision of competent authority.


         Payment of Dues

    All dues shall be paid by the dates as notified by the Principal.


         Late Fee/Fine

    If the dues (other than those payable at the time of admission) are not paid by the due date, a late payment fine of Rs. 10/- for each day after the due date (including holidays) shall be charged, subject to the maximum of Rs. 150/-only. If the fees are not paid within 15 days after due date including holidays for any reason whatsoever the name of the defaulting student shall be deemed to have been removed from the rolls of the Institute


         Payment of Fines & Dues

    Student shall pay on demand, any fine imposed on them or the cost of any damage to equipment or other property of the Institute or loss of library books. On failure to do so, the same will be deducted from their caution money, If the cost of damage to be recovered or loss exceeds the amount of caution money, the balance shall be deposited by the student on demand by the Principal. Failure to do so may result in the removal of the student's name from the institute rolls. The student shall clear all dues to the institute before they are permitted to appear in the promotional/final exam. If the dues are not cleared, their admit cards/provisional certificates/diploma will be withheld.


         Striking off the name from Rolls

    The name of the student will be deemed struck off the rolls on account of:

    1. Non-payment of tuition fee and other dues for 15 days after the last date for payment. The Principal may, however, grant not more than 10 days of grace in deserving cases on application by the parent/guardian.

    2. The name of student may also be struck off from the roll on account of:
      1. Continued absence without obtaining leave for ten consecutive working days.
      2. Irregular attendance and poor progress in studies.
      3. If ordered to be expelled or rusticated from the institute.

    Note: The parents/guardians may in their own interest keep in touch with concerned authorities regarding conduct, progress and their ward.


         Re - Admission

    On payment of re-admission fees alongwith the dues and penalty, re-admission of detained students or those whose names have been struck off may be considered at the discretion of the Principal.


         Uniform

    Wearing of the specified Uniform is compulsory during duty hours.

    • White colour suit along with white dupatta and white canvas shoes has to be worn by both ANM & GNM students for the first three months.
    • The students will be wearing specified colour suits w.e.f. November along with the Identity Card and white canvas shoes.
    • Lab coat and I.Card and watch have to be worn during the clinical duty.
    • Hair should be combed neatly. In case of long hair the hair should be made into a bun and covered with a black net.
    • Jewellery is not allowed, only small earrings can be worn by students.
    • The nails should be clean and trimmed. No nail polish and make-up should be present.
    • Students who do not adhere to the uniform policy will be asked to leave the clinical area and will be marked absent and appropriate make-up duty has to be performed. The make-up duty may be higher than the day missed and will be determined by the Principal.

         Vacations and Holidays

    Annual Vacation 30 days per annum
    Sick Leave 10 days per annum
    Prepatory Leave 7 days per annum
    For Internship Period 1 week vacation

    Note:
    • Sick leave can not be accumulated from year to year.
    • Students who miss the classes and clinical field have to make up for the loss of days from annual vacation and extra days in clinical field; otherwise she will not be allowed to sit for the examination.
