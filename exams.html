Examination & Evaluation

During first year of training programme, students will be evaluated after 12 weeks to assess the students ability to continue the course. Only those candidates who pass in this preliminary period will be allowed to continue the course.

The student who does not complete the classes and clinical field experience will not be allowed to sit for the examination.


         Internal Assessment

    Internal Assessment of 25 marks is earmarked for each subject and the criteria for internal assessment is as follow:

    1. Class & Clinical attendance
    2. Clinical Evaluation
    3. Nursing care plan
    4. Case study/case presentation
    5. Observation visit/health teaching
    6. Monthly class tests
    7. Quiz tests/Assignments
    8. Drug study
    9. Diary/I.C.U. Gadgets

         Examining Body

    Delhi Nursing Council is the examining body for admission to examination. Eligibility for Admission to Examination is strictly as per INC norms. A candidates shall be eligible for the admission to the state council/Board examination if the Principal of the school certifies that:

    1. She has completed not less than eleven months of the course in each year.
    2. She has a minimum of 75% attendance (irrespective of the kind of absence in theory and practical in each subject for appearing for examinations.
    3. She must have 100% attendance in each of the clinical area before the award of completion certificate.
    4. The overall performance of the student and her conduct during the entire academic year has been satisfactory.
    5. The student has passed in the internal assessment in each subject i.e., both in the teaching and practical by securing 50% marks separately in each theory and practical(s).
    6. The record of practical experience is complete.

    Note: The principal shall send to the council/Board the internal assessment for each subject i.e. both theory and practical(s).


         Grading of Examination

    Examination shall be graded on the aggregate marks of the programme as follow :

    Distinction 80% and above
    First Division 70% - 79.9%
    Second Division 60% - 69.9%
    Pass 50% - 59.9%
