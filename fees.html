Fees

  • Details of fee structure including admission and tuition fee, mess charges, hostel charges and clinical/hospital training transportation can be had from the office from time to time keeping in view the price index.
  • If a student fails in Council Examination and / or repeats the year, she will be charged again.
  • During the entire course of training period, the institute reserves the right to change the fee structure and it will be effective from the date as may be decided.

Note: The fees paid at the time of admission/during the course are not refundable.
