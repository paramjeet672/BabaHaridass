Principal's Desk

Mrs. K.K. Vohra

My greetings and best wishes to each student who wants to join this noble profession of nursing. We have started these courses to impart to the girl students excellent educational training and thus provide them with lots of job opportunities. The faculty at BHINE provides in depth knowledge to the students and lays more stress on practical training. Emphasis here is laid on physical, mental and moral growth of the student so as to develop a wholesome personality. We believe in blending modernity with tradition. At BHINE learning is a continuous process. We welcome you to BHINE , to a world of learning.
