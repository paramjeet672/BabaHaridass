Facilities


         Clinical Facilities

    The institute is affiliated to the following hospitals so as to provide clinical experience to the students:

    Sanjay Gandhi Hospital, Mangolpuri, Delhi-110083 300 beds
    Rural Health and Training Centre, Najafgarh, Delhi-110043
    Rao Tula Ram Memorial Hospital, Jafferpur, Delhi-110073 100 beds
    Institute of Human Behaviour and Allied Sciences, Dilshad Garden, Delhi - 110095 250 beds

         Library Facilities

    Baba Haridass Institute of Nursing Education has a newly build separate Library with 1000 books and many professional journals.

         Recreational Facilities

    • Educational trips and excursions are arranged from time to time for the students.
    • There is an exclusive recreation room for student nurses.
    • T.V., VCD, Transistors and other indoor & outdoor games are available at the Institute.
    • Separate common room, canteen services, water coolers, electricity generators and overall greenery and sufficient open space is available in the campus.
    • Students participates in various SNA activities organized throughout the year.
