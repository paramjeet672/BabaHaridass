Admissions 2017 - 2018

The addmissions for the academic year 2017 - 2018 are now open. The Application Form along with prospectus are available at the college campus on payment of Rs.500. All details regarding addmissions are mentioned below:

Courses Offered:


GNM (General Nursing & Midwifery Course)

    Duration: 3 years which includes six months internship. Students are admitted only once in a year.

    Eligibility:

    • The minimum educational requirement is class XII (pass).
    • The applicant at the time of admission must have completed the Age of 17 years and should not be above 35 years as on 31st December of the year in which admission is sought. Age relaxation for SC candidates as per rule.
    • Admission is subject to satisfactory medical examination report.


ANM (Auxillary Nurse & Midwife Course)

    Duration: 2 years. Students are admitted only once in a year.

    Eligibility:

    • 10+2 class passed or its equivalent preferably with Science subjects with aggregate of 50% marks. 5% marks relaxation for SC/ST candidates.
    • Those who have cleared 10+2 vocational ANM Course (revised after 2001) from school recognized by Indian Nursing Council.
    • The applicant at the time of admission must have completed the Age of 17 years and should not be above 35 years as on 31st December of the year in which admission is sought. For ANM/LHV, there is no age bar. Age relaxation for SC candidates as per rule.
    • Admission is subject to satisfactory medical examination report.


Admission Procedure:


         General Instructions

  • The candidates are required to obtain Admission form by paying the requisite fee.
  • The Duly filled Application Form alongwith Attested Copies of documents as per list given in the prospectus must be submitted to the Admission Office as per Admission Schedule.
  • Admit Card must be collected from the Admission Office as per Admission Schedule. Institute bears no responsibility for non-receipt of Admit Card for whatsoever reason.
  • Entrance exam will be held in the Institute as per Admission Schedule. Candidate will not be allowed to appear for the exam without the Admit Card.
  • The result will be displayed on the notice board of the Admission Office as per Admission Schedule.
  • The candidates according to their Merit will have to appear for the Interview as per the Admission Schedule.
  • On the day of Interview, the selected candidate has to complete all the requirements of the admission immediately after the interview on the same day and submit the following:
    1. Course Fee
    2. Original copies of the documents submitted at the time of application
    3. Two photographs each of four relatives who will be permitted to meet the candidate
    4. Affidavit
    5. Medical Fitness Certificate
    6. Admit Card
  • All admissions are provisional and subject to approval by Delhi Nursing Council and Verification Report from concerned examination board / council.
  • If at any level of the course, certificate/affidavit submitted by the candidate is found false, admission will be cancelled.
  • The candidateâ€™s admission can be rejected if any discrepancy is noted in the application form in age which is less even by a day, marks obtained in subjects of 10+2, which is / are less even by a fraction.
  • No candidate will be allowed to take their original certificates during course of study for any reason. If for some purpose the candidate insists on taking the original certificates for a short period they need to pay the tuition fees for the entire course in advance.

Note: If the seat is not claimed (if allotted), then it will be allotted to the next candidate.


         Check List for filling Application Form

    The candidates are advised to ensure the following points before filling the Application Forms:

    • Whether they fulfill the eligibility criteria for the Course selected.
    • Whether they have enclosed the required documents with the Application Form before submission at the Admission Office.
    • Whether they have kept a copy of the submitted documents for their own record.

         Documents to be attached with Application Form

    Attested copies of the following certificates (by a Gazetted Officer) to determine the eligibility should be enclosed with application in the order given below:
    • Certificate of Class Xth
    • Mark sheet of Class Xth
    • *Certificate of Class Xllth
    • *Mark sheet of Class Xllth
    • In case of OBC / SC / ST, copy of Community Certificate from the competent authority.
    • Residence Proof (Ration Card / Voter ID / Driving Licence etc.)
    • Certificate of Character/Conduct from institute last attended.
    • Three attested recent passport size photographs (by a Gazetted Officer) of the candidate with Name, Date of Birth printed on photo.

    * Students awaiting result of Class XII, must submit at the time of entrance exam.

Note: All Original Certificates will be checked at the time of interview.


         Additional Instructions

  • Candidates who are found to be medically fit for will be considered for admission.
  • Original documents should be produced at the time of interview. Incomplete applications' will not be considered for interview.
  • No travelling allowance will-be given to the candidates coming for the Interview or joining the school. School does not provide lodging for candidates or their relatives coming to appear for interview
  • Any student who fails to join on the prescribed date will lose her seat and that seat will then be offered to the candidate on the waiting list.
  • If provisional certificate of 10+2 is produced at the time of interview the candidate must produce proper original certificate at the time of admission.
  • If at any level of the course, certificate/affidavit submitted by the candidate is found false, she will be expelled from training and she will be liable to legal action.

         Mode of Selection

  • After scrutinizing the application forms of the candidates, only eligible candidates as per eligibility criteria will be called for written test and interview.
  • Selection will be made on the basis of marks obtained in written examination and the interview by selection committee.
  • Marks obtained in the written test will have a weightage of 70 marks.
  • Interview which will test attitude, motivation, health fitness will have weightage of 30 marks.
  • Selection will be based on the basis of merit.
  • The decision of selection committee will be final.

         Joining

  • Successful candidates have to join on date as intimated to them.
  • All the selected candidates will have to stay in the Nurse's Hostel and should bring along all articles necessary for their stay in the hostel.
  • The candidate should bring her prospectus alongwith while joining, as it contains all the rules and regulations which she has to abide by.

Fees:

  • Details of fee structure including admission and tuition fee, mess charges, hostel charges and clinical/hospital training transportation can be had from the office from time to time keeping in view the price index.
  • If a student fails in Council Examination and / or repeats the year, she will be charged again.
  • During the entire course of training period, the institute reserves the right to change the fee structure and it will be effective from the date as may be decided.

Note: The fees paid at the time of admission/during the course are not refundable.
